A Quiet Place - part II

Synopsis: A Quiet Place - Part 2, takes place shortly after the deadly events of the first film and the death of Lee Abbott (John Krasinski). The rest of the Abbott family (Emily Blunt, Millicent Simmonds and Noah Jupe) must now move on and leave their old home, facing the horrors of the world, continuing to fight to survive in silence and with a newborn baby. Forced to venture into the unknown in search of survivors, they realize that not all monsters are those that are attracted by sound.


Runtime: 97 minutes

Direction: John Krasinski

Cast: Emily Blunt, John Krasinski, Cillian Murphy

Genre: Suspense, Fantasy, Horror
