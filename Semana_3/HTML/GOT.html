Game of Thrones

Synopsis: Game of Thrones tells the story of a place where a force destroyed the balance of the seasons, long ago. In a land where summers can last several years and winters a lifetime, supernatural claims and forces flock to the gates of the Kingdom of the Seven Kingdoms. The brotherhood of the Night's Watch seeks to protect the realm from every creature that might come from beyond the Wall, but they no longer have the resources to ensure everyone's safety. After a ten-year summer, a harsh winter promises to arrive with a darker future. Meanwhile, conspiracies and rivalries run in the political game for the dispute for the Iron Throne, the symbol of absolute power.


8 Seasons

Creator: D.B. Weiss, David Benioff

Cast: Emilia Clarke, Peter Dinklage, Kit Harington

Genre: Suspense, Adventure, Medieval Fantasy, Horror
